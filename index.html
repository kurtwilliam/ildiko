Ildiko Sinka

Ms. Ildiko Sinka is a successful professional with 20+ years of experience in finance and business management. She has been instrumental in numerous executive roles within her industry and her experience ranges from business development and client relations to monitoring financial budgets and corporate services.

About

As an entrepreneurial business leader with an international background, she founded an internet financial services company, Canadian Dispute Mediation Group Inc. (CDMG). As CEO of CDMG Inc. from 2014 to 2016, she formulated and launched a business plan for shareholders, resulting in $250,000 of investment capital in order to finance the creation of a patent pending online dispute mediation model.

Prior to founding CDMG Inc., she was an executive portfolio administrator from 2009 to 2014 at Blackmont Capital Inc. At Blackmont, she had responsibility for the client/firm interface and maintained current client records and documentation, while ensuring timely and accurate processing of transactions.

She worked closely with portfolio managers and created private client financial asset allocation spread sheets for portfolio reviews. In addition to managing customer relations, she was responsible for daily distribution commission reports and transaction runs.

After a successful period at CIBC/Wood Gundy, Ildiko decided to bring her experience, industry knowledge and a significant network of contacts and associates to her new role as Private Wealth Management Coordinator at Wellington Capital Management Inc. from 2007 to 2009. This involved developing and conveying strategic financial opportunities for client groups, while also interacting with customers to market new and existing financial instruments.

From 1993 to 2000, she had many management roles at Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce (CIBC)/Wood Gundy Investment Bank Inc. Her exceptional performance and focus resulted in a fast track career progression within CIBC Wood Gundy Inc., from Executive Branch Administrator to Global Travel Executive. As Global Travel Executive, she consolidated and managed a $25 million portfolio and reported revenue share/cost savings of $5 million annually to the Executive Committee. She was a direct report to the EVP, Corporate Governance Legal Counsel of the Bank. With a global strategy, she procured and negotiated travel related contracts with major airlines, hotel chains and car rental companies headquartered in New York, London and Toronto.

During a three year period at Goldman Sachs from 1989 to 1992, Ildiko worked closely on the floor with institutional traders in day-to-day trades. Ildiko also directly managed the submission of the Bank of Canada New Issues bids for Goldman Sachs Canada and New York. She calculated closing bond figures adjustments at market close that were sent to Hong Kong, Singapore, New York and London desks. Ildiko handled institutional compliance and account settlements with custodial banks. Much of her training for this position was from the Goldman Sachs office in New York.

Her Educational background included two professional certificates in finance, attaining a Canadian Securities Course Certification and a Futures Trading Certification from the Canadian Securities Institute. Ildiko holds both a degree in Business Administration and Graphics & Advertising from Seneca College and Humber College, respectively.
